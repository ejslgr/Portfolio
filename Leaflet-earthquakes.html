Web mapping tutorial: mapping earthquakes with Leaflet

This is a web mapping tutorial I wrote and presented to the Washington Women in GIS & Technology group in September 2020. The final map that the tutorial produces (shown below) is an interactive proportional symbol map of all earthquakes that have taken place in the last 24 hours. The data is updated every minute, coming from the USGS Earthquake Hazards Program.

You can find the full tutorial on GitHub, and see a recording of my presentation to WWGT on their Vimeo channel
